Cheddar Cream Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 head broccoli chopped
  • 60g butter
  • onion, diced
  • salt,pepper
  • 40g flour
  • 55g shredded carrots
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 200g grated cheddar
  • 500ml chicken stock

Time: 30 minutes

Steps:

  1. Trim the stems off the broccoli heads and chop into small florets.
  2. In a large pot over medium heat, combine the butter and onion and sauté until translucent. Add the flour and stir until the mixture lightly browns.
  3. Stir in the half-and-half and mix until the contents reach a simmer. Turn the heat to low and add the stock. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Mix in the broccoli, carrots, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Add the cheese and stir until it melts and combines with the other ingredients.
